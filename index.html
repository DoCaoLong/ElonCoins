Life and Journey

Born on June 28, 1971, in Pretoria, South Africa, Elon Musk grew up with a passion for technology and science. After moving to the U.S., he attended the University of Pennsylvania, marking the first step in his illustrious career. From his early days with Zip2 and PayPal, Elon has continuously strived to become a symbol of innovation and perseverance.
